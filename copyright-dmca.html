Acceptable Use

You may use SaveMate only when you have the legal right to save and use the media.

Allowed examples

  • You created the content yourself.
  • You have explicit permission from the rights holder.
  • The content is public and the platform or rights holder allows you to save it.
  • You are saving content for a lawful personal use that does not infringe rights.

Prohibited examples

  • Saving or redistributing copyrighted content without permission.
  • Downloading private or restricted content that you are not authorized to access.
  • Using saved media for impersonation, harassment, doxxing, spam, or commercial misuse.
  • Removing attribution or misleading others about ownership.
  • Circumventing access controls, paywalls, login restrictions, or privacy settings.
